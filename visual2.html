Streaming Behavior by Subscription Type

Description and Interpretation

This interactive chart lets you explore how people of different ages stream music each day, broken down by subscription type and genre. You can see which genres keep listeners engaged the longest and how streaming habits change with age. It’s a fun way to understand music trends, discover what’s popular, and get insights into how fans really listen!
